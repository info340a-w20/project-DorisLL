Who should get a vaccine?

Everyone 6 months and older should get a flu vaccination to prevent the spread of illness.

Why Get a Vaccine?

The flu shot not only protecrs you from getting sick, but others who are more susceptible to the disease.

How Common is the Flu?

On average, 5%-20% of the United States will be infected by the flu.

When is the Flu Most Common?

The peak season of the flu occurs during the months of December-February.
