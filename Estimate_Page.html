How Does the Flu Affect Us?

Dangers of the flu are very commonly overlooked, due to the normalization of the illness. It is important for people to understand the need for vaccinations, in the context of how dangerous these outbreaks can actually be. Despite being an illness which is present each year, it can still have drastic effects on our community, if we forego precautionary measures.

Flu Related Hospitalizations:

Despite the current quality of health care, influenza does still have a serious effect on the health of our communities. Despite the fact that the flu is not generally considered fatal, hospitalizations occur due to the illness every year, which people do not recognize. How Long can you have the Flu without knowing?

Many people can be infecting others without knowing it. All diseases have a period of time in which a disease ie present without symptoms taking place. People will continue on without precautionary measures typically taken by an infected individual, not knowing that they are infected.
